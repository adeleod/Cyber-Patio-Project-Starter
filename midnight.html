Midnight Feast

Mixed-media stop-motion animation

One unassuming delivery man longing for a quiet shift on Christmas Eve. A series of unfortunate events. A night that just won't end. This is Midnight Feast, another chapter in the crude, higgeldy-piggeldy, stop-motion universe of friends and collaborators, Vitoria Bastos and Adele Davies.

A Christmas story commissioned by high-end fashion boutique The Place. Commissioned to create a Christmas narrative by high-end fashion shop The Place, I came together with a small team to collaborate on another paper puppet film, this time led by an unnamed Chinese delivery hero.
