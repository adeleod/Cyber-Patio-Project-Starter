The Romance of the Skeleton

An animation about the lows and lower lows of love after death. This project was a collaboration with fellow illustrator Vitoria Bastos. “O Romance de Uma Caveira” (“The Romance of the Skeleton”) is a Brazilian folk song by the duo Alvarenga e Ranchinho. The song combines satirical lyrics with a slow, romantic melody to create a tragic comedy tale. Inspired by the lyrics, we made it our goal to create a faithful visual translation of the song, using printmaking, painting, collage, drawing, and stop-motion animation.
